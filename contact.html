Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 2-4 hours during business hours, and within 24 hours for weekend messages. For urgent matters, please call us directly.

Do you offer emergency travel services?

Yes, we provide emergency travel services including urgent visa processing, last-minute flight bookings, and immediate travel assistance. Contact us directly for emergency support.

Can I visit your office without an appointment?

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure dedicated time with our consultants. You can book an appointment through our contact form or by calling us.

What languages do you support?

Our team speaks English, French, Arabic, and several local languages including Twi, Ga, and Dagbani. We're committed to serving clients in their preferred language.
