Our Application Process

1. Initial Consultation

Free consultation to assess your needs and recommend the best visa type for your purpose of travel.

Duration: 30 minutes

2. Document Collection

We provide a comprehensive checklist and assist in gathering all required documents for your application.

Duration: 1-3 days

3. Document Review

Our experts review all documents to ensure they meet embassy requirements and fix any issues.

Duration: 1-2 days

4. Application Submission

We submit your application to the relevant embassy/consulate and provide submission confirmation.

Duration: Same day

5. Status Tracking

Regular updates on your application status with proactive communication throughout the process.

Duration: Variable

6. Visa Approval & Delivery

Upon approval, we arrange visa delivery and provide final instructions for your travel.

Duration: 1-2 days

General Requirements

Essential Documents

  • Valid passport (6+ months validity)
  • Completed application form
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Proof of financial capability
  • Travel itinerary
  • Accommodation proof

Supporting Documents

  • Employment letter/invitation
  • Bank statements (3-6 months)
  • Travel insurance
  • Medical certificates (if required)
  • Educational certificates
  • Marriage/birth certificates

Additional Requirements

  • Visa fees payment
  • Biometric data (if required)
  • Interview preparation
  • Background checks
  • Language proficiency (if applicable)
  • Health examinations

Note: Specific requirements may vary based on nationality, destination, and visa type. Our experts will provide a customized checklist for your application.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does visa processing take?

Processing times vary by country and visa type. Standard processing takes 5-15 business days, while express processing can be completed in 2-5 business days for an additional fee.

What are your service fees?

Our fees depend on the visa type and destination. We provide transparent pricing with no hidden costs. Contact us for a detailed quote based on your specific requirements.

Do you guarantee visa approval?

While we cannot guarantee approval (as this depends on embassy decisions), we ensure your application is properly prepared and submitted with the highest standards to maximize approval chances.

Can you help with visa rejection cases?

Yes, we specialize in reapplication cases and can help address previous rejection reasons, improve documentation, and strengthen your new application.

Do you provide urgent/express services?

Yes, we offer express processing for urgent cases. Additional fees apply for expedited services, and processing times can be reduced significantly.

What documents do I need for my specific visa?

Requirements vary by country and visa type. After your consultation, we'll provide a customized checklist with all required documents specific to your situation.
